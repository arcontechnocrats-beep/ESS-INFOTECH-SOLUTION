About Us

ESS Infotech Solutions is a professional Information Technology and Digital Services organization committed to delivering innovative, reliable, and customer-centric solutions for individuals, businesses, institutions, and government organizations. We provide a comprehensive range of digital governance, taxation, business consultancy, banking and financial services, food safety consultancy, certification services, and citizen-centric solutions through a single-window platform.

Our mission is to simplify access to professional and government services by combining technology, expertise, and personalized assistance. We are dedicated to delivering every service with integrity, transparency, efficiency, and a strong commitment to customer satisfaction.

ESS Infotech Solutions is technically supported by Arcon Technocrats, a multidisciplinary engineering, technical, and financial consultancy organization. This strong technical association enhances our capabilities and enables us to provide integrated solutions by combining engineering excellence with modern information technology and digital governance.

With a focus on innovation, professionalism, and quality, ESS Infotech Solutions strives to provide dependable, technology-driven, and result-oriented services under one roof. Our objective is to empower citizens, entrepreneurs, businesses, and institutions by delivering efficient, transparent, and sustainable solutions that contribute to growth and development.
